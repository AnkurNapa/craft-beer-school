Refund & Cancellation Policy

Review Craft Beer School’s Refund & Cancellation Policy to understand how payments, schedule changes, and enrolment confirmations work for our online and in-person brewing programs. Our policies ensure clarity, fairness, and a seamless student experience.

  1. Course Fee Policy
    All course fees are non-refundable for both online and in-person programs. Full advance payment is required to secure your seat in any course or workshop. Fees may not be transferred between different courses once a batch has started.
  2. Session Rescheduling
    Students may request a change in batch or session dates, subject to availability in the preferred batch, advance notice before the scheduled session, and only one schedule change per enrollment.
  3. In-Person Workshop Attendance
    Students must arrange their own travel and stay for brewery sessions in Bengaluru. Missed sessions cannot be refunded; however, make-up sessions may be provided depending on availability.
  4. Course Cancellation by Craft Beer School
    In the rare event a course is cancelled or rescheduled due to operational or safety reasons, students will receive an alternate batch option, or full credit usable toward another Craft Beer School course.
  5. Eligibility & Conduct
    If a student is removed due to violation of safety or conduct rules, no refund or credit will be applicable. Students must be of legal drinking age by the start date of in-person brewing sessions.
  6. Certification Requirements
    Failure to meet attendance or assignment criteria does not qualify for refunds. Certificates are issued only upon successful completion of course requirements.
