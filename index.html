About Craft Beer School

Craft Beer School is an interactive online platform dedicated to educating and empowering the next generation of beer professionals and passionate enthusiasts.

Founded by seasoned experts with real-world experience in brewing, beer science, beer communication, and branding—including podcasting and storytelling—our school offers a dynamic space where learners can gain industry-relevant skills, share insights, and connect with fellow brewers and mentors.

From grain to glass, our curriculum covers the science, artistry, and business of beer. Whether you're looking to refine your craft, start your own venture, or explore brewing as a serious passion, Craft Beer School equips you with practical knowledge and hands-on techniques rooted in industry standards.

Join a growing community of hop-heads, brewmasters, and beerpreneurs. Here, every course is a fresh pour of expertise.
